DOES SINGLE SITTING ROOT CANAL TREATMENT HELP YOU TO CONTROL YOUR DIABETES?

Unawareness about the importance of oral health and ignorance of dental health issues which, if not taken care of, gradually lead to serious morbidity affecting the whole body. As we all know, Diabetes has emerged as a one of the biggest disease in India in past few decades due to its multi factorial etiology. We witnessed a steep rise in prevalence of diabetes from 2% to 20% in last three decades.


Diabetes is a condition in which either body does not make enough insulin or body cells are not able to utilize insulin adequately thus resulting in increased blood sugar levels(also known as hyperglycaemia). Uncontrolled hyperglycaemia cause various health problems like

  •   Damage to vessels that supply blood to vital organs, which can increase the risk of heart disease and stroke
  •   Kidney disease
  •   Vision problems
  •   Nerve problems


Tooth pain is usually acute, severe and throbbing in nature,and at times sadly leads to sleepless nights. Scientific studies have shown that regardless of the cause, pain makes diabetes self management mechanism of the body much more difficult and often leads to higher glucose levels. Even Center of disease control and prevention states that ‘even just one night of too little sleep can make your body use insulin less efficiently’.


Recurrent episodes of pain and sleepless nights might result in increase of sugar level. Most common cause of toothache is tooth decay approaching tooth nerve. If left untreated, infection doesn’t stay in tooth itself and spreads in to the periodontal ligament space and bone. In patients with low immune response, infection further progress to gums and face in the form of swelling. Increased sugar levels and its deleterious effects on gum heath are directly proportional. Scientific studies have shown that patients with poor gum health also reported with elevated sugar levels. High blood sugar may also cause dry mouth thus resulting in more tooth decay.


Root canal treatment is a procedure in which we clean the infected tooth, disinfect the root canal and seal that space by inert filling material.


Single sitting RCT vs Multiple visits RCT?


Earlier RCT was done in multiple appointments. With the advancements in technology and equipment, single sitting RCT came in to existence and is most preferred method nowadays. Studies have shown that Single sitting RCT reduces the risk of inter-appointment infection, decreases the post-procedure pain or discomfort and in teeth with apical infection increased healing rate as well as compared to multiple visits RCT.


Oral health, an inevitable part of overall healthy, should be given priority. Maintenance of oral health ensures good health of entire body. Scientific research still has to prove whether treating a tooth infection can help to control diabetes. However it’s of utmost importance to keep goog oral hygiene by self maintenance and by routine visits to the dentist.
