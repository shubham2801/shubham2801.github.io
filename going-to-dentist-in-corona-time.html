GOING TO A DENTIST – IS IT SAFE DURING CORONA TIME?

This question has emerged in mind of people suffering from dental issues with lot of if’s and but’s. Covid-19 is an infectious viral disease transmitted through respiratory droplets. The virus is also present in saliva and can be transmitted through salivary droplets.


As we all know, to break the chain of covid-19 transmission we should wash our hands with soap and water frequently, wear face masks and practice social distancing.


Toothache or tooth pain can vary from mild to severe though it can be controlled with medications most of the times. I personally don’t recommend patients to take a lot of painkillers because long term use has adverse effects on liver and kidneys. Covid-19 is an uncommon situation for all of us. We need to learn THE NEW NORMAL now. We have to learn to live with covid-19 and still protect ourselves till the vaccine comes.


Dental council of India (DCI) has issued guidelines to be followed in dental clinics to prevent the transmission of covid-19.


  •   Personal protective equipments (N95 mask, gown, head cap, gloves, face shields, shoe covers).
  •   All procedures under rubber dam.
  •   Surface disinfection with 1% sodium hypochlorite.
  •   Post treatment airborne contaminant removal protocol: Any two of the mentioned below to be followed for disinfection of clinic .
    •   UV light Disinfection.
    •   Hydrogen peroxide fumigation.
    •   Proper natural ventilation of clinic with exhaust fans.
    •   Air purifier with HEPA 13 or 14 filter.


How long can you delay treatment?


If one is not in pain, procedures like tooth whitening, tooth cleaning, implants, smile designing etc can be delayed. If you are feeling pain or discomfort you should visit dentist. Excessive usage of medicines to suppress the aetiology is not advisable.


What precautions to be taken before visiting dental clinic?


  •   Stay home, if you feel unwell.
  •   Take prior appointment over phone before visiting a dentist to avoid crowding at reception area ( Rule of one patient inside clinic at one time to be followed )
  •   Wear mask before going out for your dental appointment.
  •   Try not to accompany anyone with you for your dental appointment except for kids and differently-abled.


In one of the best dental clinics in Noida like in ours (Complete dental solutions) contactless infra red temperature check is the first step on arrival followed by hand sanitizing. You are advised to remove the mask you were wearing, sanitize hands again and wear a new mask, gloves, shoe cover and head cap provided at reception. The oxygen saturation levels in blood is also checked with pulse oximeter before entering the operatory .


Both doctor and assistant wear all personalised protective equipments for everyone’s safety. Not easy though in this hot humid weather but we do! Your safety is our first priority.


All procedures are carried out under rubber dam. Sterilized instruments in sealed pouches are used for treatment.


Toothpaste without fluoride doesn’t protect you against cavities e.g Dabur red, Meswak.


After treatment all four methods mentioned in post treatment airborne contaminant protocol is being used in our clinic to achieve optimum safety levels. It is then followed by surface disinfection and mopping of the floor by disinfectant.


It takes 30-45 minutes to prepare the clinic for next patient.


So don’t be scared in visiting dentist, we are doing everything we can to keep everyone’s safe and prevent transmission of covid-19.
